# 📊 Statistics Case Study: Crime Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a statistical case study based on a crime analysis dashboard, focusing on trends in criminal activities over recent years. The analysis highlights patterns in murder cases, crimes against women, and sexual harassment incidents to better understand growth trends and regional variations. 

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze crime trends over the past 5 years
* Identify year-wise changes in murder rates
* Examine crimes against women and harassment cases
* Compare state-wise crime distribution

## 📈 Key Insights

* Total murders over the past 5 years reached **2,90,963**, indicating a significant cumulative impact
* The current year recorded **57,582 murders**, showing an increasing trend compared to previous years
* The **average yearly murders (~48,493)** highlight a consistently high crime rate
* Crimes against women, including **5,846 murders** and **760 harassment cases**, remain a serious concern
* Certain states contribute disproportionately higher to crime statistics, indicating regional concentration
